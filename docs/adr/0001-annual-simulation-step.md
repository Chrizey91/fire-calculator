# Annual Simulation Step

The simulation advances year by year rather than month by month. Monthly inputs (Monthly Savings, Target Net Monthly Income) are multiplied by 12 for each annual step. We chose annual stepping because the inputs are naturally expressed in yearly terms (ROI, Inflation Rate, Savings Growth Rate all compound annually), the simulation horizon is 60–80 years, and annual precision is entirely sufficient for long-horizon retirement planning. Monthly stepping would add complexity with no meaningful difference in outcomes.
