I want to have a FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) calculator.
The FIRE calculator should calculate the FIRE number with the following in mind:
- the inflation rate
- the investment return
- the savings rate
- the tax rate
- the number of years to FIRE
- the expected lifespan after FIRE
- anything else needed
There should be multiple scenarios and user should be able to tweak them.
The currency shall be changable by the user.
All potential deductions should be configurable such as taxes.
There shall be a dropdown which sets some realistic defaults based on the country selected.
The FIRE calculator shall be hosted on github pages.
The website shall look modern and user-friendly.
